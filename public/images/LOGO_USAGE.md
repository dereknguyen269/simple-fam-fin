# SimpleFamFin Logo Usage Guide

## Logo Files

- **Main Logo**: `logo.png` - Full vertical logo with icon and text
- **Favicon**: `../favicon.png` - Used for browser tab icon

## Logo Description

The SimpleFamFin logo features:
- **Icon**: A creative fusion of a house and rising bar chart, symbolizing family finance growth
- **Colors**: 
  - Icon: Green (#16a34a) - representing growth and prosperity
  - Text "simple": Dark green (#15803d)
  - Text "famfin": Bright green (#16a34a)
- **Style**: Minimalist, modern, professional
- **Background**: Transparent (PNG format)

## Usage Guidelines

### Where to Use

✅ **Website**
- Header/Navigation bar
- Landing page
- Footer
- Browser favicon

✅ **Marketing Materials**
- Social media profiles
- Presentations
- Business cards
- Email signatures
- Documentation

✅ **Digital Assets**
- App icons
- Screenshots
- Promotional graphics

### Best Practices

1. **Minimum Size**: Don't scale below 32x32 pixels
2. **Clear Space**: Maintain adequate spacing around the logo
3. **Background**: Works best on white or light backgrounds
4. **Don't**:
   - Distort or stretch the logo
   - Change the colors
   - Add effects or shadows
   - Rotate the logo
   - Separate the icon from the text
